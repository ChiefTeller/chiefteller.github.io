The lecture

This is not an ordinary political lecture. It is an invitation to rethink the future.

In a unique and thought-provoking lecture, Nadav Teller presents Dual Market State theory together with ideas from Sefer Yesod and witness consciousness, offering a new way to look at the human being, society and the state in an age of accelerated change.

The lecture connects practical philosophy, systems thinking, economy, technology, leadership and consciousness. It gives the audience tools to understand not only what is changing in the world, but what new model is needed in order to deal with that change.

The audience meets the major questions of this period: why does it feel as if the old model no longer works? How does AI change the human being and the labor market? Why are the cost of living and housing prices not only economic problems, but signs of a deeper structural crisis? And how can we rethink freedom, stability, community, the state and the free market without falling back into the old fight between left and right?
